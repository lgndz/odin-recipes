Dzina's Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a faster, and less expensive lasagna while keeping the homestyle goodness we all love!

This recipe was handed down from... the internet to us circa 2021...

Ingredient

  • 1 (16 Ounce) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pouned shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
